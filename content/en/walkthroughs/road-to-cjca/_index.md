## About this series

This series documents my preparation for the **CJCA** (Certified Junior Cybersecurity Analyst) from HackTheBox Academy. Unlike the eJPT —focused on offensive exploitation— CJCA has a strong component of **defensive analysis, blue team, and incident response**.

The series includes:

- **Exploitation machines** oriented to the exam material
- **HackTheBox Sherlocks** (DFIR / blue team scenarios)
- **Professional reports** following the exam format

## About CJCA

The **Certified Junior Cybersecurity Analyst** is a HackTheBox certification focused on junior cybersecurity analyst skills, covering SOC fundamentals, traffic analysis, incident response, and professional reporting.
